Instructions for Students

This tool generates your unique attendance QR code for the College of Business Administration (CBA) Day.

  1. Enter your Student ID Number (numbers only) and Full Name (letters only) exactly as they appear in your school records.
  2. Select your Year Level, Major, and Program from the dropdown menus.
  3. Click "Generate QR Code" to create your unique attendance QR code.
  4. Download the QR code and keep it on your mobile device or print it out.
  5. Present this QR code when requested for attendance verification.

Note: Each QR code is linked to your student information. Please ensure all details are correct before downloading.
